## What is Klyme?

Klyme is an **embedded Open Banking** (often referred to as **Pay by Bank**) payment solution, that seamlessly integrates into your existing checkout without
the unnecessary need of third-party redirects or hosted payment pages.

- Putting **developers first** with our powerful **SDK**
- Payment execution using only a **single API call**
- Dedicated bank buttons for a **faster checkout experience**
- Built-in **language translations**
- Optimised **UI/UX** for all devices and browsers
