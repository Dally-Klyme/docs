## What is Klyme?

Klyme is an **embedded Open Banking** (often referred to as **Pay by Bank**) payment solution, that seamlessly integrates into your existing checkout,
eliminating the friction and outdated experience of third-party redirects and hosted payment pages.

- Putting **developers first** with our powerful **SDK**
- A **single API call** is all it takes to execute a payment
- Give your customers a **faster checkout experience** using Dedicated Bank Buttons
- Automated **Region Detection** to preload relevant banks
- Enhanced accessibility with built-in **language translations**
- Optimised **UI/UX** for all devices and browsers
